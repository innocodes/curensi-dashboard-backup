The "$10/month" figure is actually underselling your platform if you position it purely as "data". But if you position it as "Passive Income Alerts," you can charge more while still keeping a "no-brainer" entry tier.

Here is the recommended Pricing & Feature Strategy to maximize conversion for a "Cash & Carry" scanner in 2025.

The "Sweet Spot" Pricing Model
Avoid a single $10 tier. It leaves money on the table from "Whales" (who have $100k portfolios) and scares away "Minnows" (students with $500).

Use this 3-Tier Structure:

1. Free Tier (The "Hook")
   Price: $0 / month

Goal: Get them addicted to checking the site daily.

Features:

Top 5 Coins Only: Show rates for BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, DOGE.

Delayed Data: "Rates updated 1 hour ago."

Manual Refresh: User must click a button (rate limited to once per minute).

No Alerts: They have to visit the site to see opportunities.

2. "Pro" Tier (The "No-Brainer")
   Price: $9.99 / month (or $99/year)

Goal: The volume seller. Perfect for retail traders with $1k - $10k portfolios.

Features:

Unlimited Coins: Access to all 200+ perp pairs (where the 100% APR anomalies hide).

Real-Time Data: Updates every 1-5 minutes.

Email Alerts: "Notify me if SOL APR > 20%".

Liquidity Filter: "Hide coins with < $1M volume" (prevents slippage).

"Safe Trade" Calculator: Includes trading fees in the net profit calculation.

3. "Whale" Tier (The "Profit Maximizer")
   Price: $29.99 / month (or $290/year)

Goal: For users with >$10k who treat this as a business.

Features:

Instant Alerts: SMS / Telegram / Discord Webhooks (faster than email).

Historical Charts: "Show me the 30-day average APR" (Whales need stability, not just today's spike).

Z-Score Anomaly Detection: "Show me rates that are 3-sigma deviation from normal."

Spread/Basis Monitor: Warns if the entry price gap eats the profit.

API Access: Read-only API key to fetch rates for their own spreadsheets.

Why this pricing works (The Psychology)
The "10x Rule": If a user has $1,000 to invest:

A 20% APR yields $200/year.

A $10/month sub costs $120/year.

Profit: $80. The math barely works.

Correction: This is why the Free Tier is vital for small users.

The "Whale" Logic: If a user has $50,000:

A 20% APR yields $10,000/year.

They will happily pay $29/month ($350/year) to ensure they don't miss a single day of high yields.

Recommended Feature Development Priority
To justify the $9.99 Pro Tier immediately, build these 3 features first:

"Net Profit" Calculator (with Fees):

Free: Shows "20% APR".

Paid: Shows "Net Profit: $42.50 (after $10 fees)".

Why: It saves them doing math. People pay for convenience.

The "Opportunity History" Chart:

Free: Current rate only.

Paid: A line chart showing the funding rate over the last 7 days.

Why: Users need to know if the 50% APR is a "glitch" (lasted 1 hour) or a "trend" (lasted 3 days).

Email Alerts (SendGrid/Postmark):

Paid Only.

Why: FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out). "I slept and missed a 100% APR opportunity on Gate.io."

Summary
Launch with Free and $9.99 Pro. Add the $29.99 Whale tier once you have historical data (after ~30 days of running your scanner) and Telegram integration.
